Bureau of Transport Statistics

This data was sourced from the Department of Transportation's Bureau of Transport Statistics. It required merging individual datasets from the different airlines that operate out of Newark Liberty International Airport. Alongside this, the dataset was merged with other datasets to find the types of aircraft, and general information on airport performance and satisfaction.

To ensure the authenticity of the data, we ensured that the source was a reliable, audited government source. We also conducted a test where a random sample of flight numbers and dates were taken and checked to sources online to ensure accuracy

We cleaned and manipulated the dataset in the following ways:

Removed international flights due to it being out of scope.
Removed international cancelled flights due to it being out of scope.
Added new columns in the dataset to help provide more context on our insights.

Once this dataset was merged, cleaned, and new columns were added. The dataset was put into artificial intelligence tools such as Claude, Gemini, and ChatGPT to gather insights and create visualizations.

Data Source: https://www.transtats.bts.gov/
