Afternoon and Evening Shifts Need 4 to 5 More Staff to Reduce Airport Driven Delay

Our Recommendation
Newark is a complex, delay-prone airport with limited flexibility, but we can mitigate delays through better-aligned flight schedules and firmer gate-turn expectations, focused intervention on the carriers creating the most controllable delay, and stronger NAS staffing paired with shifts away from the most congested time periods.

Lessons Learned

Lessons Learned
Make sure that we are ready and capable to cover any defciencies in this project.

Our main takeaways from this project are:

Use Tableau to test out visualizations and conduct analysis.
Triple-check data accuracy at the very beginning.
Effective visualizations come from strong analysis, not the other way around.
Prioritize credible, well-structured data sources from the beginning.
GenAI is not perfect and technical knowledge is valuable for fixing mistakes.
Very large datasets make for complicated projects.
